  • TRANSFORM PUBLIC EDUCATION

    Public school teachers shoulder a monumental responsibility. We ask them to educate our next generation, but not every classroom has the resources students need to succeed.


We aim to improve our public education system by engaging citizens in an online marketplace where teachers describe, and individuals can fund specific student projects. We envision a nation where students in every community have the resources they need to learn.

By providing a simple and straightforward way for teachers and community members to bring critical, instructional resources into the classroom, we enable opportunities for achievement that might otherwise never reach underserved students.

OUR STORY IN 3 MINUTE!

Technology is a powerful force for improving people’s lives. But right now, half the world does not have access to the benefits and opportunities that technology offers. That’s why we’re committed to bring the benefits of technology to the people and organizations who need them most.

WHO WE ARE


"If you see challenges from different perspectives, how much better will the solution be?"

We’re a Tech For Good platform developed by Dil Se Education Organisation, a charitable organisation registered under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act 2001 and supported by Google for Non-profits, Microsoft Philanthropies, NASSCOM Foundation’s BigTech program in Partnership with TechSoup Global.


Through our platform, we empower public school teachers from across India to request much-needed materials and experiences for their students. We make it easy for teachers, alumni’s, individual and institutional donors to connect and help a teacher in need, moving us closer to a nation where students in every community have the tools and experiences they need for a great education.
